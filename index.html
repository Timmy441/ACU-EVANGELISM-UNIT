✝️ The Death of Jesus Christ

🏺 Trial Before Pilate

Jesus was brought before Pontius Pilate, Roman governor, who found no fault but yielded to the crowd.

"Pilate said, 'I find no basis for a charge against him.'" - John 19:6

👑 Crown of Thorns

Soldiers mocked Jesus, placing a crown of thorns and purple robe on Him.

"They struck Him on the head... spat on Him." - Mark 15:19

✝️ Crucifixion at Golgotha

Nailed to the cross at 9 AM between two thieves. Darkness covered the land from noon to 3 PM.

"It is finished!" - John 19:30

⚡ Earthquake & Temple Veil

Earthquake split rocks. Temple veil tore from top to bottom. Jesus died at 3 PM.

"Truly this was the Son of God!" - Matthew 27:54
